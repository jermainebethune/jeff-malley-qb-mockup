Meet Coach Malley

Jeff Malley played quarterback at the University of Miami during some of the program's most competitive years, learning first-hand what it takes to compete at the highest level. After his playing career, he transitioned to the coaching side as Director of Football Operations at FIU, where he worked closely with recruiting, player development, and the day-to-day operations that shape college programs.

Now, Jeff brings that insider knowledge directly to high school quarterbacks and their families. His approach combines elite mechanics training with the recruiting intelligence most families don't have access to—helping athletes build the skills and the visibility needed to earn college opportunities.

What Athletes Get

Mechanics and Fundamentals

Film Breakdown

Game film critique with clear corrections, situational decisions, and next-week priorities.

Recruiting Positioning

Parent Alignment

The Development Method

  1. 01

    Evaluate: baseline mechanics + game film audit.

  2. 02

    Prioritize: 3 performance levers for the next 30 days.

  3. 03

    Train: in-person or remote sessions with measurable reps.

  4. 04

    Position: craft recruiting narrative and exposure plan.
